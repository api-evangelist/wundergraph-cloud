# WunderGraph Cloud Plans

WunderGraph Cosmo (the managed cloud platform) offers four pricing tiers designed for
individual developers through large enterprises. Pricing is based on monthly request
volume, team size, federated graph count, and data retention needs.

## Developer (Free)

- **Price:** $0/month
- **Team size:** 1 user
- **Monthly requests:** 10 million
- **Data retention:** 7 days
- **Federated graphs:** 1
- **Max subgraphs:** 10
- **Support:** Community support
- **Features:** Basic federation, OAuth/JWT auth, subscriptions, Prometheus metrics,
  OpenTelemetry tracing

## Launch

- **Price:** $499/month
- **Team size:** Up to 10 users
- **Monthly requests:** 250 million
- **Data retention:** 14 days
- **Federated graphs:** 2
- **Max subgraphs:** 20
- **Support:** Email support
- **Features:** All Developer features plus expanded team and graph limits

## Scale (Most Popular)

- **Price:** $3,499/month
- **Team size:** Up to 25 users
- **Monthly requests:** 1 billion
- **Data retention:** 30 days
- **Federated graphs:** 5
- **Max subgraphs:** 100
- **SLA:** 99% uptime
- **Support:** Slack Connect, private Discord
- **Features:** All Launch features plus Single Sign-On (SSO), Role-Based Access
  Control (RBAC), rate limiting, persisted queries, multi-exporter observability

## Enterprise

- **Price:** Custom (contact sales)
- **Team size:** Unlimited
- **Monthly requests:** Custom
- **Data retention:** Custom
- **Federated graphs:** Custom
- **Max subgraphs:** Custom
- **SLA:** Up to 99.99% uptime
- **Support:** Dedicated solution architect, priority support, MS Teams integration
- **Features:** All Scale features plus subgraph check extensions, cache warming,
  custom MSA, dedicated cloud environment

## Special Programs

WunderGraph offers discounts for early-stage startups with limited revenue and
non-profit organizations. Contact WunderGraph directly to apply.

Source: https://wundergraph.com/pricing
